Shipping & Returns Policy

expand_more
We offer worldwide shipping with premium holographic packaging to ensure your items arrive in style. Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery for unworn items with original tags attached.

Sizing Guide for Gabrielle's Collection

expand_more
Our sizing is inclusive and designed for diverse body types. Please refer to our detailed size chart on each product page, which includes specific measurements for chest, waist, and hips to ensure a tailored fit.

International Customs Duties

expand_more
For international orders, customs duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations. These are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in the checkout price.

Care Instructions for Delicate Fabrics

expand_more
To maintain the quality of our delicate fabrics, we recommend gentle hand washing with cold water or dry cleaning. Avoid direct sunlight when drying to preserve color vibrancy.

Pre-Order Timelines

expand_more
Pre-order items typically ship within 3-4 weeks. You will receive email updates throughout the production process, bringing you along on the journey of creating your garment.
